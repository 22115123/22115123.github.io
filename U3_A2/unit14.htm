Learning Outcomes
By the end of the unit, students should be able to

Identify and describe the function of various computer hardware components.
Safely handle and install internal and peripheral hardware devices.
Configure BIOS/UEFI settings and install operating systems.
Perform routine maintenance tasks to ensure system reliability.
Troubleshoot and resolve common hardware issues.
Set up basic network connections and troubleshoot connectivity problems.
Career Pathways
Completion of this unit can lead to entry-level positions such as IT support technician, hardware technician, or computer repair specialist. It also serves as a foundation for further studies in computer hardware, networking, or IT support.
